KiDS-GGL References¶

Acknowledegements¶

If you use kids_ggl in your research, please cite Dvornik et al. (2017). You may optionally also cite Brouwer et al. (2016) and Sifon et al. (2015), for which early versions of kids_ggl were developed.

External resources¶

The development of kids_ggl has at many stages taken advantage of software developed as part of the Jupyter project.

In addition, kids_ggl uses the following Python packages, which should also be cited at appropriate places:

Standard Python packages¶

  • IPython (https://ipython.org/)
  • matplotlib (https://matplotlib.org/; Hunter 2007)
  • numpy (https://www.numpy.org/)
  • scipy (https://www.scipy.org/)

Other packages¶

  • astropy: Astronomical utility package (Astropy Collaboration 2013, 2018/website)
  • CAMB: Transfer function solver (Lewis et al. 2000/website)
  • emcee: Halo model MCMC sampling (Foreman-Mackey et al. 2013/website), which implements the Affine invariant Ensemble sampler of Goodman & Weare (2010).
  • hmf: Halo mass function (Murray et al. 2013/website)
