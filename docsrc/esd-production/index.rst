=========================
 KiDS-GGL ESD Production
=========================

The first module of ``kids_ggl`` allows the user to easily produce galaxy-galaxy lensing measurements around an existing sample of 
lens galaxies, supplied by the user. Summaries of the process of calculating the galaxy-galaxy lensing signal can be found in
|ref:viola15|_ and |ref:dvornik17|_.
